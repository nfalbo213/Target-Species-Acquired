About

Who we are

TSA was born in John Prioli's garage in August 2021. John is an avid bass fisherman who's been targeting Largemouth and Smallmouth bass for close to 25 years.

The goal of TSA Lures is to create realistic paint schemes and colorways that imitate a wide variety of baitfish and most importantly, provide fishermen and women with patterns that WORK.

The blanks used are all alternatives to big name bait manufacturers and have been tested to ensure the action is equally as good as those from the original factories. TSA proudly uses only razor sharp Gamakatsu hooks and Spro Power split rings so there is never a need to replace hooks right out of the box. #getthenet
